# Brand Overview

## Mission

Make Web3 ownership real, rewarding, and easy to understand through a product people already buy every day.

## Vision

Become the bridge between Web2 and Web3 through energy, rewards, ownership, and community.

## What We Are

- Premium Energy Drink Brand
- Rewards Ecosystem
- Community-Driven Platform
- Ownership-Focused Consumer Brand

## What We Are Not

- Investment Platform
- Get Rich Quick Scheme
- Gambling Product
- Speculative Crypto Project

## Tagline

Drink it. Own it.

## Brand Mantra

Fuel the grind. Own the upside.
