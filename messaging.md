# 💬 Messaging Framework


Our messaging should be simple, consistent, and easy to understand.

Whether someone is discovering NFT Energy for the first time or has been part of the community from day one, the message should always be clear.

---

## 🎯 Core Message

> Every can earns more than energy.

This is the foundation of everything we communicate.

NFT Energy is more than a beverage.

It's a product, a community, and an ecosystem built around participation.

---

## 🥤 Customer Message

### What We Want Customers To Understand

```text
Buy a drink.
Earn rewards.
Join the community.
```

Simple.

No complicated explanation required.

---

## 🐰 Holder Message


### What We Want Holders To Understand

```text
Own a Wabbit.
Help shape the future.
Unlock ecosystem benefits.
```

Wabbits are more than collectibles.

They represent participation in the NFT Energy ecosystem.

---

## 🤝 Partner Message


### What We Want Partners To Understand

```text
Get featured.
Reach new audiences.
Build together.
```

NFT Energy creates opportunities for communities to gain real-world exposure.
