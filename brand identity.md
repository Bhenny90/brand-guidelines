# 🎨 Brand Identity

Our visual identity is designed to be bold, recognizable, and instantly connected to the NFT Energy ecosystem.

Every design choice should reinforce energy, ownership, community, and innovation.

---

## 🏷️ Logo Philosophy

The NFT Energy logo represents the product.

The Energy Wabbits logo represents the community.

Together they create a bridge between the physical and digital worlds.

> One brand.
>
> One community.
>
> One ecosystem.

## 📸 Visual Style

Our visuals should communicate movement, energy, and progress.

### Preferred Content

- Product Photography
- Community Events
- Builders & Creators
- Gamers
- Entrepreneurs
- Real Customer Experiences

### Avoid

- Generic Stock Photos
- Corporate Imagery
- Overly Complex Graphics
- Cluttered Designs

---

## 🐰 Energy Wabbits Standards

The Wabbits are a community asset.

Every depiction should feel recognizable and consistent.

### Do

✅ Full outfits

✅ Streetwear

✅ Jerseys

✅ Builder culture

✅ Confident poses

✅ Adult proportions

---

### Don't

❌ Child-like characters

❌ Four-legged animals

❌ Hyper-realistic fur

❌ Violent imagery

❌ Gambling imagery

❌ Inappropriate content

---

## 🖼️ Design Principles

Everything we create should follow these principles:

### Bold

Create attention.

### Clean

Keep messaging simple.

### Consistent

Maintain brand recognition.

### Product First

The drink should always remain the hero.

### Community Focused

Showcase real people and real participation.

---

## 📋 Brand Checklist

Before publishing any design, ask:

- Does it feel like NFT Energy?
- Is the product visible?
- Is the message clear?
- Is the design easy to understand?
- Would someone recognize the brand instantly?

If the answer is yes, you're on the right track.
